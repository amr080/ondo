Ondo Finance Expands Institutional Reach with OUSG Deployment on the XRP Ledger

Ondo Finance

Jan 28, 2025 2 min

Ondo Finance will deploy Ondo Short-Term US Government Treasuries (OUSG), its tokenized US Treasury fund, on the XRP Ledger (XRPL), a decentralized layer-1 blockchain. This deployment, set to go live within the next six months, will bring OUSG to the XRPL's broad institutional user base, introducing a composable and compliance-first asset to a blockchain built for financial institutions. This integration establishes an avenue for institutional DeFi and advanced cash management solutions, bringing DeFi and traditional finance closer than ever.

Key Highlights of the Deployment

  • Bringing OUSG to the XRPL: Institutional investors on XRPL will, for the first time, have access to OUSG, a product combining security, compliance, and yield, backed by a high-quality US Treasuries fund and stringent KYC/AML standards.
  • Established Liquidity: Ripple and Ondo have secured commitments to seed OUSG liquidity upon initial deployment, driving further adoption of tokenized RWAs on the XRPL.
  • 24/7 Mint and Redemption Features: A key feature of OUSG on the XRPL is going to be seamless interoperability with Rippleâ€™s RLUSD stablecoin. This integration will allow instant minting and redemption of OUSG via RLUSD, empowering users with round-the-clock liquidity and financial flexibility.

OUSG delivers a compliant, yield-bearing investment product backed by the BlackRock USD Institutional Digital Liquidity Fund (BUIDL) and high-quality short-term US Treasuries. The XRPL, renowned for its focus on traditional finance and featuring deep institutional relationships, provides the ideal infrastructure for integrating tokenized RWAs.

The deployment of OUSG on the XRPL brings a host of advantages:

Native compliance features on the XRPL, such as Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) and onchain credential management, enhance regulatory adherence.
  • Accessible Settlement and Liquidity: OUSG and RLUSD offer high-quality, low-risk liquidity options. The 24/7 intraday settlement breaks free from traditional trading hours, enabling efficient portfolio management and greater access to tokenized US Treasuries-backed assets.

The XRP Ledger is uniquely tailored to the needs of traditional financial institutions. With over a decade of experience powering cross-border payments, digital asset custody, and stablecoin issuance, the XRPL offers an unparalleled foundation for RWA tokenization and institutional DeFi.
