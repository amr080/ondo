🌌 USDY Goes Live on Noble, Expanding to the Cosmos Ecosystem

USDY, our market-leading yieldcoin, is now live on Noble, making it accessible to the 90+ chains in the Cosmos ecosystem. This launch introduced the first tokenized treasuries product to the Cosmos ecosystem, furthering Ondo’s mission to make institutional-grade financial products and services available to everyone. Continue Reading.

⚫️ USDY Launches on Aptos Alongside Major Integrations

The launch of USDY on Aptos brings the first native US Treasuries yieldcoin to the Aptos ecosystem, including integrations with three of the largest protocols, Aries Markets, Thala, and LiquidSwap. USDY serves various use cases on Aptos, including enhancing liquidity pools by providing a natively yielding asset for trading pairs, serving as collateral on money markets to enable capital efficient borrowing and lending activities, and being used as collateral for Collateralized Debt Positions and derivatives. Continue Reading. 

💸 Arbitrum DAO Votes to Allocate 17% of Treasury Diversification to USDY

The Arbitrum DAO ratified the STEP Committee's recommendation to diversify 6M ARB from the Arbitrum DAO Treasury into USDY. This represents over 17% of the total 35m ARB allocation, second only to BlackRock's BUIDL. This vote grants the DAO access to one of the industry's top yield-generating real-world assets. Continue Reading.

🟣 Pyth Network Introduces USDY/USD Price Feed Across 65+ Blockchains

The launch of the Pyth USDY/USD price feed expanded the reach of USDY to the EVM, Move, Bitcoin, and Cosmos ecosystems, with several ecosystem participants already integrating this price feed, including Drift, FlashTrade, Thala, and DePerp. Continue Reading.

🌐 Ondo Ecosystem Expansion

The Ondo Ecosystem Directory – a list of the institutions, protocols, and networks aligned with our mission of delivering institutional-grade financial products onchain continues to expand. Noble, Pyth Network, Dolomite, and Mango Markets joined the Ondo Ecosystem this month, among others. Commenting on their respective integrations:

Mike Cahill, CEO and Co-Founder of Douro Labs, said, "We’re excited about the launch of the USDY/USD price feed. The Pyth contributors are committed to providing feeds for leading real-world assets based on the demands of Web3 developers, and as Ondo Finance and USDY continue to gain momentum, launching this price feed was a clear choice.”
Corey Caplan, Creator of Dolomite, stated, “We’re excited about the addition of USDY and the new opportunities it will unlock for our users. We believe USDY is an exciting new foundational asset for DeFi and we are proud to further expand the utility of USDY via our next-generation money market.”

🗣️ Ondo Featured in Uphold Institutional’s Token Discovery Series Webinar

Ian De Bode, Chief Strategy Officer at Ondo Finance, joined Uphold Institutional's Token Discovery Series and covered Ondo’s mission, momentum, the RWA tokenization landscape, and how the Ondo Ecosystem is bringing institutional grade financial products & services to everyone.
