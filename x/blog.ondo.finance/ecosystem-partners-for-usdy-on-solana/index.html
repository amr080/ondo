Ondo is thrilled to have launched USDY on the Solana blockchain. Now, users across the Solana ecosystem can easily access native USDY through a growing array of dApps, or by onboarding with Ondo. This launch is the result of a collaborative effort involving prominent ecosystem partners, including Jupiter Exchange, Raydium Protocol, Orca, Kamino Finance, Meteora AG, deBridge Finance, Squads, and Switchboard.

USDY aims to provide users with the utility of stablecoins while offering an impressive 5.2% APY. These strategic partnerships are well-positioned to not only facilitate its broad distribution within the Solana ecosystem but also to usher in an era of advanced yield farming strategies.

Recap on USDY

USDY is the worldâ€™s first tokenized note secured by a bankruptcy-remote portfolio of US Treasuries. It is accessible to non-US individual and institutional investors and is designed to combine the accessibility of stablecoins with high-quality, US dollar-denominated yield. Key features include:

Broadly Accessible: Tokenized loan that can trade globally in secondary markets to non-US investors

Yield-Bearing: Accrues a variable interest rate, adjusted monthly by the issuer. Yield accrues in the form of a redemption value that increases over time

Daily Liquidity: Non-US investors can both mint and redeem USDY on a daily basis

High-Quality Collateral: Secured by a portfolio of short-term US Treasuries under control agreements

Compliant with Regulations: Issued under a continuous Reg S offering to offshore investors with best practices KYC/AML/CFT procedures

Bankruptcy-Remote: Assets designed to be ring-fenced from a hypothetical Ondo bankruptcy
